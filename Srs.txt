Purpose:The purpose of this document is to provide a detailed specification for the development of ChatApp, chat application that allows users to communicate in real-time through text, multimedia, and group chats.

Product Functions:
1.User registration and authentication
2.Contacts
3.One-on-one and group chats
4.Multimedia sharing
5.Emojis and stickers for expressive communication

User Interfaces:
1.Login and registration screens
2.Contacts list
3.Chat interface
4.logout
5.change password
